Selfie ‚Äî Close vs Far (same face size)

Two portraits with the face kept the same size: (A) close + wide vs. (B) farther + zoomed. Close distance exaggerates near features; stepping back restores natural proportions.

Why

  • Perspective comes from distance. The closer the camera, the bigger near features appear.
  • Focal length changes field of view; moving the camera changes geometry.
